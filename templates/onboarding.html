  1. You and your peers are going to be presented with a logic task
  2. First, you will solve the task by yourself. After doing so, wait for the rest of your group to solve the task as well. This may take a few minutes (often less than 1 minute, but sometimes 3-5 minutes).
  3. After everyone submits the task, a chat box will appear. You are expected to discuss the solution of the task.
  4. Give your best effort in solving the task, responding to the questions and suggestions of the other participants. In previous studies, groups that collaborate well - discussing their arguments and solutions among each other - are typically better in solving the task.
  5. Everyone who solves the task correctly will be granted a bonus.
  6. If the chatbox does not appear for more than 10 minutes after you solve the task by yourself, you may submit the HIT. Submitting before that will result in rejection.
